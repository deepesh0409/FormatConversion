About FormatConverter

Learn more about the team, mission, and technology powering our platform.

Our Mission

FormatConverter was built in 2026 with a single, clear mission: to make digital file management completely frictionless. We believe that editing a PDF, resizing an image, or removing a background shouldn't require expensive software subscriptions, forced sign-ups, or invasive data tracking.

Built for Speed and Privacy

Based in India, our team of developers combined the raw speed of Python's FastAPI with the intelligence of modern AI neural networks. The result is a platform that delivers studio-quality conversions directly in your browser.

Your Data is Yours

We built FormatConverter on a "Zero-Trust" privacy model. Your files are processed in isolated memory buffers and are automatically, permanently wiped from our servers immediately after your download completes. We don't read them, we don't store them, and we don't train AI on them.
